Research topics

My main interest is studying states of consciousness using the tools of cognitive neuroscience as well as phenomenology. [Under construction]

Curriculum

Since April 2013, I'm a post-doctoral fellow at the University of Geneva, Switzerland, in the Laboratory for Neurology and Imaging of Cognition (LabNIC). I'm in charge of developing research on the hypnotic state using fMRI and EEG .

I've defended my PhD thesis in September 2012. I prepared it at CerCO-CNRS UMR5549 in Toulouse, France.

I've first studied cognitive sciences in France ( University of Lumière-Lyon 2)

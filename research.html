My Research

I focus my research on investigating how . I have started with the study of mind wandering and meditation using mainly EEG. I then used fMRI to study the state of hypnosis. I am now moving on combining EEG and TMS to study the neural basis of vivid mental imagery, that I consider as being a core element of mind wandering, meditation and hypnosis.

To study these states of consciousness, I try to combine both cutting-edge approaches in neuroimaging and collection of first-person data.
